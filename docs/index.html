Tanos Lamborghini Urus

FORMACAR HAS CRAFTED ONE OF THE MOST EXTENSIVE AND TECHNOLOGICALLY INTRICATE RESTYLING KITS FOR THE LAMBORGHINI URUS

This aerodynamic package consists of 42 pieces. Among the primary components are the rear bumper with a diffuser, the front bumper, a hood, and a comprehensive expansion that incorporates the replacement of the front fenders.

Clients can choose between a kit fabricated from ultra-high-temperature fiberglass (Fiberglass) and an exclusive version made of carbon fiber featuring a visible weave pattern (Visible Carbon).

Components manufactured from ultra-high-temperature fiberglass match the weight of original parts, boast a thickness of 2-3 mm, and can endure temperatures up to 120°C, all while maintaining robust durability.

Parts made from carbon fiber are produced using methods akin to aviation technologies. They offer superior strength, can withstand temperatures beyond 120°C, and are lighter compared to the original components. Additionally, the carbon fiber lends the Italian SUV an aesthetic appeal that's crucial for luxury vehicles.

Diamant GT Mercedes-Benz AMG

FORMACAR HAS EXPANDED ITS COLLECTION WITH THE DIAMANT GT PROJECT BASED ON THE Mercedes-AMG GT 63 S MODEL (THE RESTYLING IS COMPATIBLE WITH VERSIONS 3.0, 4.0, 53, 63, AND 63 S)

The complete restyling kit includes 40 components. A distinctive feature is the diffuser with a centrally located exhaust tip in the style of the AMG GT Coupe, as well as a double spoiler on the trunk lid for the 63 S version. The hood and front bumper give the car an added sense of aggression and character.

Customers can choose an aerodynamic kit made from ultra-high-temperature fiberglass or an exclusive version made of carbon fiber with a unique design. The fiberglass components have a weight equivalent to the original parts and a thickness of 2-3 mm, and are able to withstand temperatures up to 120°C. The exclusive carbon fiber parts are manufactured using techniques akin to aviation standards and boast higher durability, a temperature threshold above 120°C, and a lighter weight compared to the original components.

M-Power BMW 8

Speed, drive, outstanding dynamic performance and impeccable design – the above pretty much sums it up for the BMW M850i xDrive Grand Coupe (G16) equipped with M8-POWER aftermarket body kit.

Included in the kit are 12 new parts that complement the car body perfectly, including, a front splitter, front bumper side details, front bumper middle detail, rear bumper side details, rear diffuser, rear bumper aerodynamic add-ons, diffuser reflector, side sill splitters, a window-mounted spoiler, a trunk lid spoiler, wheel arch flares, and a hood cover.

The M8-POWER is available in two versions: ultra-high-temperature glass-fiber reinforced plastic (GFRP) and exclusive carbon-fiber edition (CFP) complete with a unique pattern.

The GFRP components weigh about the same as the OEM parts, are 2-3 mm thick as per the standard, support operational temperatures up to 120°С and are extremely durable.

The exclusive CFP version has properties on par with those of aeronautical industry products, boasts a high durability ratio, supports operational temperatures in excess of 120°С, and weighs less than the original M850i body kit does.
